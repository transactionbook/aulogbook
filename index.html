Free km tracking — forever

The core function is free for life. No time limit. No credit card. No "free trial". Drive, track, export — for free.

AutoStart — drives itself

Auto-starts a shift when you drive 10+ km/h for 20 seconds. No need to remember to tap "Start".

🛰️

Survives Uber & Didi

Background tracking via Android's foreground service keeps recording when you switch apps or lock the screen.

🛢️

Auto toll + airport detection

Detects M5, NorthConnex, M2/M4/M7/M8, Lane Cove, Eastern Distributor, WestConnex + all AU airport zones. One-tap pre-fill.

🔗

Toll Sync (E-Toll) · Pro

Import the official E-Toll CSV — auto-matches each charge to the right trip by time + road. Flags unreimbursed tolls.

📷

Scan receipts with smart detection

AuLogbook reads date, total, litres, rate, GST and ABN. Verify screen flags low-confidence fields so you catch errors.

📊

Export to spreadsheet · Pro

Multi-tab XLSX workbook per year (Q1–Q4 + Year Summary, photos bundled). Drop straight into your BAS workflow.

🚗

Multiple vehicles · Pro

Private plate some nights, rented EV on others? Maintain separate logbooks per vehicle with per-shift assignment.

🔔

Service + rego reminders

Set service intervals (3 months / 10,000 km) and rego expiry per vehicle. Never miss a renewal again.

📋

ATO-compliant logbook

Captures the continuous 12-week log the ATO requires. Detects your best business-use-% window (valid 5 years).

💰

Auto business / private %

Commute legs = private, between-jobs driving = business. The logbook-correct split, automated — no spreadsheets.

☁️

Personal cloud backup

Back up to a folder you choose — Google Drive, Dropbox, anywhere. Survives uninstall; restore on a new phone.

📤

Export to email / share

CSV, PDF, daily CSV, JSON backup — all shareable via your email, cloud drive, WhatsApp. Your data is portable.

🌓

Light + dark mode

Auto (follows system), Light, or Dark. Pick whatever suits your shift — and switch any time.

🔒 Your data never leaves your phone

No accounts. No servers. No analytics. No ads. Everything AuLogbook records stays in app-private storage on your device. We don't see your trips, your receipts, your E-Toll login, or your Uber/Didi fares — ever.
