# Play Console → App content → Content rating (IARC questionnaire)

The IARC (International Age Rating Coalition) questionnaire asks ~12 yes/no
questions about content. For AuLogbook, the answer to almost all of them is NO.
Your app will receive: **PEGI 3 / Everyone / G** (the lowest rating).

---

## Question 1: Does your app contain violence?

**NO** (no cartoon, fantasy, real, or stylised violence)

## Question 2: Does your app contain sexual content or nudity?

**NO**

## Question 3: Does your app contain profanity or crude humour?

**NO**

## Question 4: Does your app contain references to drugs, alcohol, or tobacco?

**NO**

## Question 5: Does your app contain gambling?

**NO** (no real-money gambling, no simulated gambling)

## Question 6: Does your app contain paid digital goods that simulate gambling?

**NO** (the Pro subscription is for productivity features only)

## Question 7: Does your app contain horror or fear themes?

**NO**

## Question 8: Does your app contain user-generated content that may not be suitable for all ages?

**NO** (user-generated content is tax records and receipt photos — boring but
not inappropriate)

## Question 9: Does your app contain chat or social features?

**NO**

## Question 10: Does your app share the user's location with other users?

**NO** (location is collected but NEVER shared — see Privacy Policy)

## Question 11: Does your app contain unrestricted web browsing?

**NO** (the only outbound link is to the E-Toll login site and Play Store subscriptions management)

## Question 12: Does your app contain digital purchases?

**YES** (Pro subscription via Google Play Billing — $4.99/month or $39/year)

## Question 13: Does your app share user information with third parties for advertising?

**NO** (no ads, no advertising SDKs, no third-party data sharing)

## Question 14: Does your app use persistent IDs for advertising purposes?

**NO** (no advertising identifier use, no analytics)

## Resulting classification

| Region | Rating |
|---|---|
| Australia (ACB) | **G** (General) |
| Europe (PEGI) | **3** |
| USA (ESRB) | **E** (Everyone) |
| Germany (USK) | **0** |
| Brazil (ClassInd) | **L** |
| South Korea (GRAC) | **All** |

These are the most permissive ratings possible. AuLogbook will be available to
all users in all regions where Play Store operates.

## Target audience (Play Console → App content → Target audience)

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Target age groups | 18-24, 25-34, 35-44, 45-54, 55+ (all adults) |
| Exclude children under 13? | YES (the app is for working adults) |
| App is directed at children? | NO |

The "directed at children" question matters: answering NO here means your app
isn't subject to COPPA / GDPR-K restrictions on children's data — important
for keeping the data-flow simple.
