## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
Rideshare AuLogbook is the all-in-one tax logbook app built specifically for Australian Uber and Didi drivers. It auto-tracks every kilometre with GPS, scans fuel and service receipts with on-device OCR, syncs your toll charges against E-Toll statements, and produces the ATO-ready reports your accountant actually wants.

★ THE FEATURES ★

▸ 100% FREE KM TRACKING — FOREVER
  The core function (GPS trip tracking + autostart + ATO logbook) is free for life.
  No time limit. No credit card. No "free trial". Drive, track, export — for free.

▸ AUTOSTART — DRIVES ITSELF
  Auto-starts a shift when you drive 10+ km/h for 20 seconds. No need to remember
  to tap "Start" before every shift.

▸ BACKGROUND TRACKING THAT SURVIVES UBER & DIDI
  Switch to Uber or Didi mid-shift and AuLogbook keeps recording. Uses Android's
  foreground service (the same mechanism Uber uses), so tracking doesn't pause when
  the screen is off or you've switched apps.

▸ AUTO TOLL + AIRPORT DETECTION
  Detects when you cross a toll gantry (M5, NorthConnex, M2, M4, M7, M8, CCT, Lane
  Cove Tunnel, Eastern Distributor, WestConnex, Military Road E-Ramp) or enter an
  airport zone (Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide). One-tap pre-fill for
  fixed-amount tolls; "check tag statement" hint for variable tolls.

▸ TOLL SYNC — AUTO-MATCHES E-TOLL CHARGES (PRO)
  Download your official statement CSV from Transport NSW E-Toll, import it, and
  AuLogbook matches each toll charge to the right trip by time + road. Flags tolls
  Uber or Didi may not have reimbursed you for (you have 10 days to chase them up).

▸ SCAN RECEIPTS — ON-DEVICE OCR
  Point your camera at a fuel or service receipt. ML Kit auto-crops, enhances, and
  reads the date, total, litres, rate, GST and ABN — then shows you a verify screen
  with confidence flags on fields that need a second look.

▸ EXPORT RECEIPTS TO SPREADSHEET (PRO)
  Multi-tab XLSX workbook (one .xlsx per calendar year, Q1–Q4 + Year Summary, photos
  bundled). Drop straight into your BAS workflow or send to your accountant.

▸ MULTIPLE VEHICLES (PRO)
  Driving for Uber on a private plate some nights and a rented EV on others? Maintain
  separate logbooks per vehicle, with per-shift assignment.

▸ SERVICE + REGO REMINDERS
  Set service intervals (every 3 months or 10,000 km) and registration expiry per
  vehicle. AuLogbook reminds you before they're due — never miss a rego renewal again.

▸ ATO-COMPLIANT LOGBOOK METHOD
  Captures the continuous 12-week log the ATO logbook method requires. Detects your
  best business-use-% window (valid 5 years once established).

▸ AUTO BUSINESS / PRIVATE % CALCULATION
  Home legs (commute) = private, non-deductible. Between-jobs driving = business,
  deductible. AuLogbook computes the split automatically based on a home location
  you set — the logbook-correct calculation, no spreadsheets.

▸ PERSONAL CLOUD BACKUP
  Back up your entire logbook (trips + receipts + images) to a folder you choose —
  Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, anywhere SAF supports. Survives uninstall; restore
  on a new phone with zero re-OCR.

▸ EXPORT TO EMAIL / SHARE
  CSV, PDF, daily CSV, JSON backup — all shareable via your email, cloud drive,
  WhatsApp, anything. Your data is portable, never locked in.

▸ LIGHT + DARK MODE
  Auto (follows system), Light, or Dark. Pick whatever suits your shift.

★ NO ADS · NO ACCOUNTS · NO SERVERS ★
```
