## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
Rideshare AuLogbook is the tax logbook app built specifically for Australian Uber and Didi drivers. It auto-tracks every kilometre with GPS, scans fuel and service receipts with on-device OCR, syncs your E-Toll toll charges automatically, and produces the ATO-ready reports your accountant actually wants.

★ WHY DRIVERS USE IT ★

▸ Auto-trip tracking that actually works in the background
  Switch to Uber or Didi mid-shift and AuLogbook keeps recording. Uses Android's
  foreground service (the same mechanism Uber uses), so tracking doesn't pause when
  the screen is off or you've switched apps.

▸ Receipt scanner with ML Kit OCR
  Point your camera at a fuel or service receipt. AuLogbook auto-crops, enhances,
  and reads the date, total, litres, rate, GST and ABN — then shows you a verify
  screen with confidence flags on fields that need a second look. 99% accurate
  once confirmed.

▸ Toll Sync — auto-match E-Toll charges
  Download your official E-Toll statement CSV from Transport NSW, import it, and
  AuLogbook matches each toll charge to the right trip by time + road. Flags tolls
  Uber or Didi may not have reimbursed you for (you have 10 days to chase them up).

▸ ATO-ready reports
  Cents-per-km method AND logbook method (with best 12-week window detection).
  Quarterly BAS workbooks for your accountant. CSV, PDF, and multi-tab XLSX exports.

▸ Home-leg intelligence
  Knows the difference between your commute (private, non-deductible) and
  between-jobs driving (business, deductible) — the logbook-correct split,
  automated based on a home location you set.

▸ Multi-vehicle support (Pro)
  Driving for Uber on a private plate some nights and a rented EV on others?
  Maintain separate logbooks per vehicle.

★ NO ADS · NO ACCOUNTS · NO SERVERS ★

Everything AuLogbook records stays on your phone in app-private storage. We don't
have user accounts. We don't run analytics. We never see your trips, your receipts,
your E-Toll login, or your Uber/Didi fares. Cancel your subscription any time and
your data is still yours — export to CSV / XLSX / PDF / JSON and walk away.
```
